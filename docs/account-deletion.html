Talk Mate

Account and Data Deletion

This page explains how to delete your Talk Mate account and associated data. It is the official account deletion resource for Google Play.

Last updated: February 22, 2026 Developer: ojuni Support: ojvni.dev@gmail.com

Delete in the app (recommended)

  1. Open Talk Mate and sign in to your account.
  2. Open the left drawer and tap your account profile card.
  3. Tap the trash/delete icon at the top-right of the account menu.
  4. Confirm the warning dialog to complete account deletion.
Permanent action: this cannot be undone.

What data is deleted

  • Account profile tied to your Talk Mate sign-in.
  • Conversation sessions and conversation messages.
  • Voice quota and usage records.
  • Push notification tokens associated with your account.
  • Billing entitlement snapshot linked to your user ID.

What may be retained briefly

  • Operational and security logs for abuse prevention and compliance.
  • Encrypted infrastructure backup snapshots for a limited lifecycle.
Backup/log retention target: up to 30 days before automatic overwrite/removal.

If you cannot access the app

Email support from the same email you used in the app and request account deletion. Include "Talk Mate Account Deletion Request" in the subject.
